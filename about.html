Our Story

Founded in 2017, CoffeeBliss started as a small roastery with a simple belief: great coffee brings people together. We partner with sustainable farms, roast carefully, and brew with attention to detail. Our vintage interior and relaxed vibe are designed for conversations, study sessions, and cozy afternoons.
